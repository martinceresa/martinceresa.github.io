Lean Games -- 2024

This a current project I am working at Arbitration Games Proofs.

The idea is to design a language to have authenticated data structures plus arbitration games in a single place.

Arbitration Games come from the world of Optimistic Rollups and a first idea is to replicate some of their work, but having formal proofs showing that: + Honest claim proposers can always defend their claims and win. + Honest challengers always win and losing claims are removed.
