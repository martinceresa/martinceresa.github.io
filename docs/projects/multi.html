Multi Formal Playground -- 2022

Multi is a small Coq Library implementing (a probably outdated) Tezos computation model. We were playing with several additional mechanisms that could have been added to the blockchain and we wanted to see (and prove) how these mechanisms would impact contract interactions.

We also believed that attacks (or exploits) came from the interaction of (provably) correct smart contracts at the interface definition level. In other words, we wanted to see how interaction breaks smart contracts implicit protocols or assumptions.
